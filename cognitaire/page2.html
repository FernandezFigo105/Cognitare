HOLLANDS TEST

Assessments using Holland Personality Styles link vocational interests to job families. Using two or three letter codes it provide information on the relationship between job personalities and key characteristics, college majors, hobbies, abilities, and related careers.

DIFFERNTIAL APTITUDE TEST

The DAT is a tool that measures your abilities and skills, such as verbal, numerical ability, abstract reasoning, mechanical reasoning and space relations. The DAT is used with adults looking at a wide range of career and planning options, including some industrial firms..

BIG FIVE PERSONALITY TEST

The ocean personality test is based on the five-factor model, an empirical concept in psychology that measures five pre-dominant personality traits: openness, conscientiousness, extroversion, agreeableness, and neuroticism, making the acronym OCEAN.
